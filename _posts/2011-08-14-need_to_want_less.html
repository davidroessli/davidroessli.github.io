While randomly browsing my Sunday feeds, I rediscovered Erin Hanson's brilliant «NEED TO WANT LESS» set of relevant visual philosophical statements.

I feel I want it, but do I really need it?

Gee, I recognise myself in several of them. What about you? Now is a good time to face, confront and try to pacify want we want vs what we need.
