--- layout: post title: New tools for your iMusic date: '2003-01-13 21:42:33 +0100' excerpt: 'Two new cool tools for your iPod: iTrip, a clip on FM transmitter from Griffins, and iCommune, an iTunes plug-in to stream your iTunes library.' mt_id: 362 blog_id: 11 post_id: 362 basename: new_tools_for_your_imusic categories: - daily --- Discoveries of the day: two new hot accessories for your iPod: Hardware iTrip, from Griffin Technology is a FM transmitter to listen to your MP3s on your FM receiver. Apparently, you can choose the wavelength among the entire FM spectrum (a plus on former products). Its look is too cool ..
Software iCommune, an iTune plug in to stream your music to other iTune users on the net - and not only to your local network (goes further than RendezVous discovery). I'll try that one no later than tomorrow morning!
iCommune is a plug-in which extends Apple's iTunes software to share music over the network. Your friends' music libraries appear in the iTunes source list. You can browse their collections, and choose to download or stream their music. It also allows you to make your own music library available to others.
