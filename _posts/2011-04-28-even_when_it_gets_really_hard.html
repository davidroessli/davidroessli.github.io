I've just finished reading Merlin Mann's «Cranking» entry posted on 43 Folders and feel deeply moved by the emotions it conveys.

Merlin has such a way with words. The real life images he depicts in his post are somehow hardwired to my memories, and trigger strong emotional responses.

  • Especially if you are a dad and you love your dad;
  • Especially if you do what you do because you love what you do;
  • And more so if you are self employed.

Thank you Merlin for sharing those moments with us, for finding the right words that express what a lot of us feel and experience and love and wish for.

I would have loved writing something like that.
